Our story

Your Trusted Towing Partner in Dallas

To provide fast, fair, and professional towing and roadside assistance to every customer, every time.

Express Towing Inc. was founded in 2005 with a single truck and a commitment to helping stranded drivers in Dallas. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service towing company known for our quick response times and transparent pricing. Our team has decades of combined experience in the towing industry.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of flatbeds and wheel-lift trucks, serving both residential and commercial clients. We're proud to be a locally owned and operated business that treats every customer like a neighbor.
